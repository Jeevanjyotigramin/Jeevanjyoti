About Us

Mobile no. 9823397879 | 9866244760

Jeevan Jyoti Gramin Bidhyut Sahakari Sastha Ltd. is a cooperative society established to provide electricity to rural communities. Our mission is to bridge the energy gap, promote sustainable development, and enhance the quality of life in rural areas.

We focus on renewable energy sources to provide eco-friendly electricity solutions.

Our cooperative model empowers local communities to manage their energy needs.

We ensure consistent and affordable electricity supply to rural households.
